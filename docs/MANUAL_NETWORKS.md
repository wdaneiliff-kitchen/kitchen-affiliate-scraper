# Manual Login & Data Pull – Remaining Networks

These affililate networks cannot be scraped by scripts because they contain complex login flows, such as logging in with an email link or an unsolvable captcha. Use the steps below to log in and pull commission/creator data manually.

---

## Refersion

Refersion uses **reCAPTCHA + email magic link**: enter your email, solve the captcha, then click the login link sent to your inbox.

### 1. Volair

- **Login URL:** <https://www.refersion.com/affiliate/login>
- **Steps:**
  1. Open the login URL, enter username and password from [Notion](https://www.notion.so/Affiliate-Partners-1e04b3848bfc8098b7e1dad02417fca2).
  2. Open your email and click the Refersion login link.
  3. Once in the dashboard, navigate to **Commissions** (<https://www.refersion.com/affiliate/commissions>) and export or copy the data you need (e.g. date range, export CSV if available).
- **Note:** Same Refersion domain as ERNE; use the correct affiliate account/email for Volair.

---

### 2. Gearbox

- **Login URL:** <https://gearbox.refersion.com/affiliate>
- **Steps:**
  1. Open the URL (Gearbox uses the **gearbox** subdomain).
  2. Enter the affiliate email for Gearbox.
  3. Solve the reCAPTCHA and submit.
  4. Click the login link in your email.
  5. In the dashboard, navigate to **Commissions** (<https://gearbox.refersion.com/affiliate/commissions>) and export or copy the commission data.
---

### 3. ERNE

- **Login URL:** <https://www.refersion.com/affiliate/login>
- **Steps:**
  1. Open the URL in your browser.
  2. Enter the affiliate email for ERNE.
  3. Solve the reCAPTCHA and submit.
  4. Click the login link in your email.
  5. Navigate to **Commissions** (<https://www.refersion.com/affiliate/commissions>) and export or copy the data.
- **Note:** Same Refersion domain as Volair; use the ERNE affiliate account/email.
